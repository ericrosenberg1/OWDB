Welcome to OWDB is Jericho!

The Open Wrestling Database (OWDB) is a community built wrestling database. Every piece of data has been added by our amazing community dating back to 2024. If you smell what OWDB is cookin'!

W OWDB

Why OWDB?

We created OWDB to help track wrestling history for fans, by fans. Whether you're researching classic WrestleMania moments, tracking your favorite indie wrestler's career, or building an app that needs wrestling data, OWDB has got you covered.

Our plan is simple: make OWDB open and usable for other purposes with a powerful API. Anyone can contribute on GitHub or through OWDB's website to make it more awesome. If you ain't down with that, we got two words for ya: JOIN NOW!
