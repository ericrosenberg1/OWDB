W OWDB

Why OWDB?

We created OWDB to build the ultimate wrestling encyclopedia—complete, rich, and comprehensive. Whether you're researching classic WrestleMania moments, tracking your favorite indie wrestler's career, or building an app that needs wrestling metadata (like Wrestlarr), OWDB has got you covered.

We continuously aggregate data from many authoritative sources to ensure every wrestler profile, every match result, and every championship reign is as complete as possible. The data is open and accessible through a powerful API. If you ain't down with that, we got two words for ya: JOIN NOW!
